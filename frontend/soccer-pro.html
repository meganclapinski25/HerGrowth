Professional Women's Soccer

The National Women's Soccer League has played a major role in the professionalization of women's soccer in the U.S., growing from a modest launch in 2013 into a league with record attendance, major broadcast deals, and rising investment. Today, the NWSL represents one of the strongest and fastest-growing professional women's sports leagues in the world.
